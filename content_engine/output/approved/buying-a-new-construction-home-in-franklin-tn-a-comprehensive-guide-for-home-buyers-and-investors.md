**How to Buy a New Construction Home in Franklin**

While buying a new construction home can seem intimidating, it's easier than you think. Here are some steps to follow:

1. **Research neighborhoods**: Look into local schools, community amenities, and nearby attractions.
2. **Get pre-approved for a mortgage**: Talk to your lender about getting pre-approved for a mortgage before starting your search.
3. **Find the right real estate agent**: Work with an experienced agent who knows the Franklin market.
4. **Visit model homes**: Tour model homes in different neighborhoods to get a feel for the area.
5. **Make an offer**: When you find the perfect home, make an offer that's competitive and reasonable.

**Tips for First-Time Home Buyers**

As a first-time home buyer, it's essential to be prepared for the process. Here are some tips:

* **Get your finances in order**: Make sure your credit score is good, and you have enough savings for a down payment.
* **Be patient**: Buying a new construction home can take time, so don't rush into anything.
* **Ask questions**: Don't be afraid to ask your agent or builder about any concerns you may have.

Some common mistakes first-time home buyers make include:

* Not getting pre-approved for a mortgage
* Not researching neighborhoods thoroughly
* Not asking enough questions
