**Top-Rated Public Schools in Williamson County**

When it comes to public education, Williamson County has a reputation for excellence. Here are some of the top-rated elementary and high schools:

*   **Clovercroft Elementary School**: With a student-teacher ratio of 17:1, Clovercroft is an excellent choice for families with young children.
*   **Jordan Elementary School**: This school boasts a strong curriculum and dedicated teachers, making it a popular option among parents.
*   **Lipscomb Elementary School**: Located in the heart of Franklin, Lipscomb offers a well-rounded education and a supportive community.

**High Schools**

*   **Franklin High School**: As one of the top-rated high schools in Williamson County, Franklin High prepares students for success in college and beyond.
*   **Independence High School**: With a focus on STEM education, Independence High is an excellent choice for students interested in science, technology, engineering, and math.
*   **Centennial High School**: This school offers a comprehensive curriculum and a variety of extracurricular activities, making it a great option for students with diverse interests.

**Private Schools in Williamson County**

While public schools are excellent, many families prefer the smaller class sizes and personalized attention offered by private institutions. Here are some top-rated private schools:

*   **Donelson Christian Academy**: Located just outside Franklin, DCA offers a comprehensive curriculum and a supportive environment for students with diverse learning styles.
