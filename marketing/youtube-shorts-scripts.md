## Production Notes

- **Format:** 9:16 vertical (1080x1920) for YouTube Shorts
- **Length:** 15-30 seconds each (under 60 sec max)
- **Captions:** Always on — 80% of mobile viewers watch without sound
- **Phone number:** Always visible on screen the entire video
- **CapCut workflow:** Record → import → auto-captions → add phone number text overlay → export
- **Upload:** YouTube Shorts + can repurpose for Instagram Reels
