# PART 1 — REUSABLE MONTHLY NEWSLETTER TEMPLATE

Copy this structure each month. Replace `[PLACEHOLDER]` markers. Keep it warm and useful — community + tips first, listings second. Aim for a 60/40 split: value content to promotion.

---

### Subject lines (pick ONE; rotate styles month to month)

Provide 3–5 options per issue and A/B test the top two. Keep under ~45 characters so they don't truncate on mobile.

1. **`[Month] Middle TN home prices — what changed`**
   *Curiosity + local relevance. "What changed" implies news the reader doesn't have yet. Strong for past clients who track their own equity.*
2. **`Your [City] home is worth this in [Month]`**
   *Personal + value. "Your home" + a dollar implication drives the highest open rates with past clients (self-interest beats news).*
3. **`5 things happening in Middle TN this month`**
   *Listicle + community. Lower-pressure, high open rate; good when the month is light on market drama.*
4. **`Thinking of selling before fall? Read this`**
   *Question + urgency. Best when targeting the lead segment more than past clients.*
5. **`[Neighborhood] just had a record sale 👀`**
   *Specific + intrigue. Use only when you have a real notable sale to anchor it (no invented numbers).*

> **Open-rate guidance:** Personal/"your home" subject lines and short curiosity gaps consistently beat salesy ones. Avoid ALL CAPS and words like "FREE," "guaranteed," and excessive punctuation (spam triggers). One emoji max.

After **every closing**, send a short standalone "Just Sold in [City]!" email to the past-client/sphere segment (separate from this newsletter).
